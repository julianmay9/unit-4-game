Instructions

The player will generate a random target number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals displayed as buttons on the page.

When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.

The player wins if their total score matches the random target number from the beginning of the game.

The player loses if their score goes above the random number.

The game restarts whenever the player wins or loses. When the game begins again, the player will need to generate a new random number. Also, all the crystals will have four new hidden values.
